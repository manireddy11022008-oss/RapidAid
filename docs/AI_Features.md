# AI Features

## Overview

RapidAid leverages Artificial Intelligence (AI) to enhance emergency response, improve decision-making, and optimize healthcare coordination. AI helps reduce response time, recommend suitable hospitals, and provide intelligent assistance during emergencies.

---

## AI-Based Hospital Recommendation

Instead of selecting the nearest hospital, the AI evaluates multiple factors such as:

- ICU bed availability
- Trauma care facilities
- Specialist doctor availability
- Medical equipment
- Distance and travel time
- Current emergency load

The system recommends the most suitable hospital for the patient's condition.

---

## Intelligent Ambulance Routing

AI identifies the nearest available ambulance and calculates the fastest route using real-time traffic information.

Benefits:

- Reduced response time
- Improved ambulance utilization
- Faster patient transportation

---

## Emergency Severity Assessment

Based on user input (text, voice, image, or video), AI estimates the severity of the emergency.

Severity Levels:

- Critical
- High
- Medium
- Low

Critical cases receive the highest priority for ambulance dispatch.

---

## Speech-to-Text Conversion

Users can report emergencies using voice. AI converts speech into text, enabling quick emergency reporting in multiple languages.

---

## Multi-Language Translation

AI translates emergency messages between different languages, allowing patients and healthcare providers to communicate effectively.

Supported languages include:

- English
- Telugu
- Hindi
- Tamil
- Kannada
- Malayalam
- Marathi
- Bengali
- Gujarati
- Punjabi
- Odia
- Urdu

---

## AI Health Assistant

RapidAid includes an AI-powered chatbot that can:

- Answer basic health-related questions
- Guide users during emergencies
- Recommend nearby healthcare facilities
- Provide first-aid information (informational only)

---

## Predictive Resource Allocation

AI analyzes emergency trends to help hospitals prepare by predicting resource requirements such as:

- ICU beds
- Ambulances
- Emergency staff

---
